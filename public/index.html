    Is this safe to use?

    The page is static and open source. It never sees your seed phrase. Every transaction is built locally, simulated against algod first to catch failures, then handed to your wallet to review before you sign.

    The App IDs shown on each position match the contracts Cometa deployed. Verify any of them on allo.info before signing.

    Why does my withdraw fail?

    Usually it’s a contract lock period that hasn’t elapsed, or not enough ALGO to cover the inner-transaction fee bump (~0.004 ALGO). The simulate step catches both and shows the on-chain error before you sign anything.

    How it works

    1. Connect your Algorand wallet (or paste an address to look up first).
    2. We scan the blockchain directly for any Cometa positions on your address.
    3. Click Withdraw on a position. Your wallet will ask you to sign — review the amount, then approve.
    4. Cometa's contract sends your stake and rewards back to your address. Done.
    Is this safe?

    This page never sees your seed phrase. It only constructs transactions that call Cometa's existing on-chain contracts — the same contracts the official site uses. Your wallet will show every detail of every transaction before you sign.

    Before you sign anything, check that the App ID in your wallet matches the one shown on this page. You can verify any App ID against allo.info.

    What if my withdrawal fails?

    A few common reasons: your position is still in its lock period, the network is congested, or you don't have enough ALGO to pay the network fee (~0.004 ALGO). Try again, or file an issue.

    Why is this needed?

    Cometa's official frontend has been unreliable. The smart contracts on Algorand are still live and working — they just need a working interface to talk to them.
